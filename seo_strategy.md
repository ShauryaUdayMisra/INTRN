# SEO Strategy

## In scope
- Public marketing and discovery pages
- Internship search and internship detail pages
- Blog listing and blog post pages
- Public company information pages
- Shared crawlability files such as `robots.txt`, `sitemap.xml`, favicon, and AI-crawler guidance files

## Out of scope
- Authenticated student dashboard routes
- Authenticated company dashboard routes
- Authenticated admin routes and internal tooling
- Private API endpoints used only after login

## Target audience
- High school students looking for internships
- Parents, educators, and counselors evaluating youth internship options
- Companies interested in posting internships for high school students

## Primary keywords
- High school internships
- Internships for high school students
- Student internships
- Youth internships
- Internship opportunities for teens
